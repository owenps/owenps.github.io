Latest Work

Rhino Chess

[Dec. 2020] A Deep Learning Chess Engine implemenation based on the 2016 paper "DeepChess: End-To-End Deep Neural Network for Automatic Learning in Chess". There the authors introduce a novel approach to the architecture of Chess Engines. In short, we teach a network to considers two positions and output the better one. With this, we can manipulate the alpha-beta search algorithm to make decisions based on a deeper understanding of the classic board game.
